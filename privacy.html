Privacy Policy.

Last updated: 18 July 2026. This page explains what tejaskannadiga.com collects, why, and how you can control it.

1. Who this policy covers

This Privacy Policy applies to tejaskannadiga.com (the "Site"), operated on behalf of Tejas S in connection with the YouTube channel and program "The Devil's Advocate." It does not cover third-party platforms such as YouTube, Instagram, or Threads, which have their own privacy policies.

2. Information we collect

The Site is a static informational website. We only collect information you choose to give us:

  • Contact form details. If you use the contact form, your name, email address, selected reason, and message are prepared into a pre-filled email opened in your own mail application. This information is not transmitted to or stored on any server operated by this Site — it is sent directly from your device to our inbox only if and when you choose to send that email.
  • Direct contact. If you email or call the addresses listed on the Contact page, we receive whatever information you choose to share in that message or call.
  • Basic technical data. Like most websites, the hosting provider may automatically log standard technical information (such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited) for security and performance purposes.

3. How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries, interview requests, collaboration proposals, and corrections submitted through the Contact page.
  • To maintain and secure the Site.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties.

4. Cookies and analytics

The Site does not set its own tracking cookies. If analytics tools (for example, to understand overall visitor numbers) are added in the future, this policy will be updated to describe them before they go live.

5. Third-party links

The Site links out to YouTube, Instagram, and other third-party platforms. Once you leave tejaskannadiga.com, that platform's own privacy policy applies to your activity there — this Site has no control over, and is not responsible for, those platforms' data practices.

6. Data retention

Messages sent to us by email are retained only as long as needed to respond to and, where relevant, follow up on your inquiry.

7. Your choices

  • You are never required to use the contact form — you may reach out by phone or email directly instead.
  • You may request that we delete correspondence you've previously sent us by emailing the address below.

8. Children's privacy

The Site is intended for a general audience interested in political and civic commentary and is not directed at children under 13.

9. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the Site or applicable law. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.
